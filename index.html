Biography

Dr. Srichandan Sobhanayak is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Bhubaneswar. With a strong academic background and years of research experience, he has contributed significantly to the fields of distributed systems and network communications.

His research focuses on developing efficient protocols and architectures for next-generation networks, including IoT, VANETs, and Edge Computing environments. He is actively involved in mentoring students and leading research projects aimed at solving complex engineering problems.
